FAQ

Is RupiRamp custodial?

No. Users keep control of their crypto until they convert with licensed off‑ramp partners.

How long do payouts take?

Beta target is 2–10 minutes after on‑chain confirmation and KYC approval by our partners.

What are the fees?

Our platform fee is shown upfront in the Transak widget. FX and partner fees are included in the quote.
